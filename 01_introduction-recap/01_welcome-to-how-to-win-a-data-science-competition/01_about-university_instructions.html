Consistently ranked as one of Russia’s top universities, HSE University is a leader in Russian education and one of the preeminent economics and social sciences universities in eastern Europe and Eurasia. Having rapidly grown into a well-renowned research university over two decades, HSE University sets itself apart with its international presence and cooperation.

Now a dynamic university with four campuses operating in Moscow, St. Petersburg, Nizhny Novgorod and Perm, involving over 47 000 students incl. 5000+ foreign students, and 7 000 academic staff representing 100+ countries, and 140+ research institutes and centers and 46 international labs.

Our faculty, researchers, and students are dedicated to maintaining the highest academic standards. Our newly-adopted structural reforms support HSE University’s drive to internationalize and conduct groundbreaking research.

HSE University is a leader in combining Russian education traditions with the best international teaching and research practices. HSE University offers outstanding educational programmes from secondary school to doctoral studies, with top departments and research centres in a number of fields.

HSE engages in cooperation with 400+ universities world-wide through bi- and multilateral projects, European Commission and other schemes, international associations and networks memberships.

Since 2013, HSE University has been a member of the 5-100 Russian Academic Excellence Project , a highly selective government programme aimed at boosting the international competitiveness of Russian universities. HSE development strategy-2030 is aimed to establish at HSE a leading world-class scientific and educational, analytical and consulting centre.

HSE University is ranked among the top 100 institutions worldwide in Politics & International Studies, Sociology, Economics & Econometrics, History, Social Policy & Administration in the QS – World University Rankings by Subject, and is among top-performing participants of the “5-100” Russian Academic Excellence Project .

According to the results of 2020, HSE University ranks third in Russia in terms of total number of positions in subject and faculty world rankings; HSE University places second for number of positions in the Top-100; In more than half of its offered subject areas, HSE University ranks first among all Russian academic institutions; In nine subject areas, HSE University is the only Russian institution to be represented in the global rankings; HSE University entered the rankings in five subject areas for the first time last year.
